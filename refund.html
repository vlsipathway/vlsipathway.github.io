Cancellation and Refund Policy

Overview

VLSI Pathway is committed to providing high-quality digital learning experiences. Since our courses are digital products delivered online, we have implemented a clear and fair refund policy to protect both our students and our business interests.

Quick Summary: Refunds are only available within 2 days of purchase if the purchase was made by mistake and no course content has been accessed.

Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if all of the following conditions are met:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 2 days (48 hours) of the original purchase date
  • You have not accessed any course content or downloaded materials
  • The purchase was made by mistake or you purchased the wrong course
  • Your payment has been successfully processed

Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  • Send an email to vlsipathway@gmail.com with your order details and reason for refund
  • Include your name, email address, and transaction/order ID
  • Our team will review your request and respond within 2-3 business days
  • Upon approval, the refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • Refund processing typically takes 5-7 business days depending on your bank

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following scenarios:

  • You have accessed course content, viewed videos, or downloaded materials
  • Your refund request is submitted after 48 hours of purchase
  • You have completed course modules or lessons
  • The course has been shared with other individuals
  • You have obtained course access through promotional offers or discounts
  • The refund request is made for reasons other than purchase mistakes

Course Access After Purchase

Once you have paid for a course and accessed the content, your access to the course materials will be valid for life. However, no refunds will be issued after this point, regardless of the reason.

Duplicate Purchases

If you have accidentally made duplicate purchases of the same course, please contact us immediately at vlsipathway@gmail.com. We will review your case and may provide a refund or transfer the duplicate purchase to another course.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy or need to request a refund, please reach out to us:

  • Email: vlsipathway@gmail.com
  • LinkedIn: VLSI Pathway

Policy Updates

VLSI Pathway reserves the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
