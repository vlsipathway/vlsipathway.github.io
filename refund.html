Refund and Cancellation Policy

Our policy ensures fairness and transparency regarding the purchase of digital educational products.

1. Refund Eligibility (Digital Courses)

Due to the immediate digital nature of our course materials (videos and downloadable content), **all sales are final** once the course access has been granted (i.e., once the customer receives their login key/link).

A refund will be considered ONLY if:

  • The course content is technically inaccessible due to an error on our part, and the issue cannot be resolved within 7 business days of notification.
  • The course was purchased by mistake, and the customer **has not logged in** or **accessed any course content**.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

We **do not** offer refunds in the following circumstances:

  • If the user has accessed or completed any portion of the course material.
  • If the refund request is submitted more than **7 days** after the date of purchase.
  • If access is revoked due to violation of the Terms and Conditions (e.g., sharing login credentials).

3. Cancellation

Course enrollment cannot be cancelled once the payment is processed and access is granted.
