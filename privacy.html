VLSI Pathway Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how VLSI Pathway collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website, programs, and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you enroll in a course, register an account, or use our services. This includes:

  • **Personal Identification:** Name, Email Address, and Phone Number (collected during payment via Razorpay).
  • **Transaction Data:** Details regarding the course purchased (e.g., LTSpice Course) and payment status.
  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you access and use our secure course pages (e.g., login attempts, browser type).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected primarily for the following purposes:

  • To **Process Transactions** and fulfill your course access immediately after payment.
  • To **Grant and Manage Secure Access** to the private video content.
  • To communicate with you regarding course updates or technical support.
  • To fulfill our legal obligations regarding payment processing (Razorpay/KYC).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share information with:

  • **Payment Processors (Razorpay):** Necessary to complete the financial transaction. Razorpay handles all sensitive payment information (e.g., UPI details).
  • **Legal Authorities:** When required to comply with law, regulation, or legal process.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.
