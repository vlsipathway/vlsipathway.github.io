Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all learners, instructors, and users who access or interact with our Services. It covers the personal and non-personal information collected while you browse, register, or participate in courses offered through the platform.

Your information is used solely for purposes related to providing educational content, maintaining platform performance, and enhancing user experience. We may share limited information with:

Data Security

We follow standard technical and administrative measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. While no system is completely secure, we continually monitor and improve our security practices. Users are encouraged to keep their login credentials confidential and log out after accessing shared devices.

You can review, update, or delete your account information at any time by contacting us directly. You may also choose to opt out of marketing emails or newsletters. Some data may remain in backup or archive systems for compliance or record-keeping purposes.
