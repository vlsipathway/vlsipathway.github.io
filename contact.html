FAQs

When will I receive a response to my email?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. Technical support may receive priority depending on urgency.

Can I call VLSI Pathway for support?

Currently, we provide support through email and LinkedIn. Email is our primary support channel and ensures we can track your inquiry effectively.

What information should I include in my email?

Please include your full name, email address, course name (if applicable), order ID, and a detailed description of your issue or question. This helps us resolve your query faster.

How do I access course materials after purchase?

After payment, you'll receive an email with login credentials. Visit vlsipathway.com/courses/login.html, enter your details, and access your courses from your dashboard.
