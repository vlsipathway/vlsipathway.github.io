Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

When will I receive a reply to my query?

We usually reply within 2–3 working days. Technical support requests are prioritized based on urgency.

Can I reach VLSI Pathway by phone?

At present, we provide support only through email and LinkedIn to maintain proper documentation and ensure timely follow-up.

What details should I include in my email?

Include your full name, registered email, course name (if applicable), and a clear description of your issue or question. This helps us resolve it faster.

How do I access my courses?

After enrollment, you’ll receive login credentials via email. Visit vlsipathway.com/courses/login.html and access your learning dashboard using your registered email ID.
