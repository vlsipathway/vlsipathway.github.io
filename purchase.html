Simple Purchase Guide

Ready to master VLSI design? Follow these simple steps to enroll in a course and gain instant access to your learning path on VLSI Pathway.

  • Step 1: Choose Your Course Explore our courses on the vlsipathway.com. Click on any course to review the detailed curriculum, project list, and prerequisites to find the program that best fits your career goals.
  • Step 2: Proceed to Payment On the course page, click the "Enroll Now" button. You will be redirected immediately to the secure payment processing page to finalize your purchase.
  • Step 3: Complete Secure Checkout Fill in your details and complete the transaction using any major credit/debit card or standard payment methods. Once payment is successful, you will proceed to the next step.
  • Step 4: Receive Credentials via Email Immediately after a successful payment, you will receive an "email confirmation" which includes your receipt and your "login credentials" (username and password) for the Student Dashboard.
  • Step 5: Log In to Your Dashboard Click on the "Student Login" button on our website (usually in the header). Use the credentials received in your email to securely log in to your personalized dashboard.
