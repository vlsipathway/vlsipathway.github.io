Course Purchase Guide

Getting started with your VLSI learning journey is simple. Follow these quick steps to enroll in a course and access your personalized learning dashboard on VLSI Pathway.

  • Step 1: Explore and Select a Course Visit www.vlsipathway.com and browse through our available programs. Each course page provides details about the syllabus, tools covered, projects, and learning outcomes — helping you choose the one that aligns with your academic or professional goals.
  • Step 2: Click “Enroll Now” Once you’ve selected a course, click on the “Enroll Now” button. You’ll be redirected to a secure checkout page to confirm your enrollment.
  • Step 3: Complete Secure Payment Fill in your details and complete the transaction using a major debit/credit card or other supported payment options. All transactions are processed through trusted gateways to ensure your security.
  • Step 4: Receive Confirmation Email After a successful payment, you’ll receive a confirmation email containing your receipt and login credentials for your VLSI Pathway account. Please check your inbox (and spam folder) for this email.
  • Step 5: Access Your Dashboard Go to the “Student Login” section on our website and enter your credentials. Your personal dashboard will display all your enrolled courses and resources.
  • Step 6: Begin Learning Start exploring your course modules, video sessions, and lab materials. Apply your knowledge through hands-on projects and exercises designed to help you master key VLSI concepts. Welcome to your learning journey! 🚀
