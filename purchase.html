Simple Purchase Guide

Ready to master VLSI design? Follow these simple steps to enroll in a course or access your chosen learning path on VLSI Pathway.

  • Step 1: Explore Courses Browse our catalog on the Courses page. Read the descriptions, check the curriculum, and review the prerequisites to find the program that best fits your goals.
  • Step 2: Add to Cart (or Enroll Now) Once youâ€™ve selected a course, click the **"Enroll Now"** or **"Add to Cart"** button on the course description page. You can add multiple courses before proceeding.
  • Step 3: Review Your Order Go to your cart to review the selected items. If you have a coupon code, enter it here and click "Apply" to adjust the total price. Ensure all details are correct.
  • Step 4: Checkout and Payment Click "Proceed to Checkout." You will be prompted to either log in to an existing account or create a new **VLSI Pathway Student Account**. We accept all major credit/debit cards and other standard payment methods.
  • Step 5: Confirmation and Access After successful payment, you will receive an **email confirmation** with your receipt and login details. The purchased course will be instantly available in your **Student Dashboard** when you log in.
  • Step 6: Start Learning! Log in and navigate to your dashboard to access all video lectures, lab materials, projects, and mentorship resources. Happy learning!
