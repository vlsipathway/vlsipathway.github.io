Shipping and Delivery Information

Digital Delivery Only

All VLSI Pathway programs and resources are delivered digitally. We do not ship any physical items, products, or printed materials. Upon completing your purchase, you’ll receive immediate access to all learning content via our secure online platform.

Quick Start: Once your payment is confirmed, an automated email will be sent containing your login credentials and access instructions — allowing you to start learning right away.

Instant and Lifetime Access

After enrollment, learners receive:

  • Instant access to all digital materials and videos
  • Lifetime access to purchased courses
  • Multi-device compatibility – access via desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • 24/7 availability to learn at your convenience

Access Instructions

  1. Complete payment securely through Razorpay or another supported gateway.
  2. Receive your confirmation email instantly after successful payment.
  3. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for login details and access links.
  4. Visit vlsipathway.com/courses/login.html to sign in.
  5. Use your credentials to access your dashboard and begin your course.

Included in Your Course

  • Comprehensive video lectures and tutorials
  • Downloadable resources and notes (where applicable)
  • Practical, project-based learning structure
  • Instructor support (where included)
  • Certificate of completion after course completion
  • Lifetime access for continuous learning

Delivery Time

All VLSI Pathway courses are delivered instantly. Access is granted immediately after payment is processed, which generally takes only a few seconds or minutes.

System Requirements

To access and complete your courses effectively, ensure you have:

  • A stable internet connection
  • An updated web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.)
  • A compatible device (desktop, laptop, or mobile)
  • Minimum 100 MB of available storage (for downloadable materials)

Access Issues

In case of login or course access issues, please reach out to us at vlsipathway@gmail.com with your order details. Our support team will respond within 2–3 business days.

Device Compatibility

Our platform is designed to be fully responsive and works across devices:

  • Desktop/Laptop: Complete access to all features
  • Tablet: Smooth and optimized layout
  • Mobile: Easy, responsive interface for mobile learning

Data and Content Security

All digital content is securely hosted and delivered using encrypted channels. We employ trusted third-party payment and hosting services to ensure your personal and transaction data remain safe. Course content is for educational use only and must not be redistributed.
