About VLSI Pathway

VLSI Pathway was founded with a simple purpose — to make the journey into chip design more accessible, practical, and engaging for learners at all levels. We bridge the space between classroom knowledge and the hands-on skills expected in today’s semiconductor industry.

Our learning paths are built using a mix of open educational resources, real-world project ideas, and engineering experience. Whether you are a student exploring analog design for the first time or an engineer seeking to strengthen your fundamentals, VLSI Pathway provides the right blend of academic depth and practical exposure.

Our Mission

To create a learning ecosystem that connects academic understanding with practical chip design experience. We aim to help students and early-career professionals build confidence in circuit design, simulation, and analysis through structured, project-based learning and accessible mentorship from practicing engineers.

What Makes VLSI Pathway Different?

  • Balanced Curriculum: Designed to suit both students and working engineers, combining theoretical clarity with real-world problem-solving.
  • Learning from Practicing Engineers: Courses and sessions are shaped by professionals who share insights from their work in the semiconductor field.
  • Hands-on Learning: Each course emphasizes simulations, lab-style projects, and documentation that help you build an authentic design portfolio.
  • Flexible and Accessible: Learn at your own pace, from anywhere, without compromising your university or work schedule.

Our Commitment to You

At VLSI Pathway, we believe that anyone with curiosity and consistency can learn chip design. Our focus is to guide learners from basic concepts to implementation-level understanding while encouraging creativity, documentation, and independent exploration.
